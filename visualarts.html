Since I got my first camera in 2015, photography and videography has been a huge part of my life. I discovered the pure joy of composing a frame, clicking the shutter button and producing a photograph. I believe photography is such an art that allows you to weave such visual stories that nothing else can. I am a huge fan of subtle moody edits, which shows all over my Instagram account.

Slowly the interest also shifted towards videography, cinematography and cinematics. Peter McKinnon was one YouTuber who inspired me a lot in this field. Hig vlogs, photos, edit style, colors fascinated me. He was actually the primary influence which pushed me to start making vlogs.

I started my YouTube channel way back in 2015, right after I learnt how to beatbox. The primary motive was to upload beatboxing videos. Gradually I started uploading videography and cinematic stuff, and invested time in color grading, transitions and visual story-telling. The mutual liking for videography and travel drove me to make travel vlogs. Recently I also upload a lot of study abroad videos, documenting my masters journey from India to USA.

To me my YouTube channel is like a time capsule, which holds video memories of every important event that happens in my life. Few years down the line, I can look back on these memories and relive them.
